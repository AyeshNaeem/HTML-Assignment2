Beef Boti Roll

Ingredients:

Prepare Beef Tikka Boti:

  • -Boneless beef preferably undercut ½ kg
  • -Dahi (Yogurt) ¼ Cup
  • -Kacha papita (Raw papaya) paste 1 tbs
  • -Adrak lehsan paste (Ginger garlic paste) 1 tbs
  • -Hari mirch (Green chilli) paste 1 tbs
  • -Lemon juice 2 tbs
  • -Kashmiri lal mirch (Kashmiri red chilli) powder 1 tsp
  • -Haldi powder (Turmeric powder) ½ tsp
  • -Dhania powder (Coriander powder) 2 tsp
  • -Lal mirch powder (Red chilli powder) 1 tsp or to taste
  • -Garam masala powder 1 tsp-Namak (Salt) 1 tsp or to taste
  • -Lal mirch (Red chilli) crushed 1 tsp
  • -Zeera powder (Cumin powder) ½ tsp
  • -Olper’s Tarrka Desi Ghee 2 tbs
  • -Hara dhania (Fresh coriander) chopped 1 tsp
  • -Koyla (Charcoal) for smoke
  • -Rolled dough
  • -Olper’s Tarrka Desi ghee ½ tbs
  • -Olper’s Tarrka Desi ghee 1-2 tbs

Praper Mayo Sauce:

  • -Dahi (Yogurt) ½ Cup
  • -Mayonnaise ¼ Cup
  • -Tomato ketchup ¼ Cup
  • -Lehsan paste (Garlic paste) 1 tsp
  • -Lal mirch powder (Red chilli powder) ½ tsp or to taste
  • -Zeera (Cumin seeds) roasted & crushed ½ tsp
  • -Namak (Salt) ¼ tsp or to taste

Assembling:

  • -Pyaz (Onion) rings
  • -Green chutney

Directions:

Prepare Beef Tikka Boti:

-Pound beef with the help of meat mallet. -In a bowl,add beef,yogurt,raw papaya paste,ginger garlic paste,green chilli paste,lemon juice, Kashmiri red chilli powder,turmeric powder,coriander powder,red chilli powder,garam masala powder,salt,red chilli crushed,cumin powder & mix well,cover & marinate for 1 hour. -In a frying pan,add desi ghee & let it melt. -Add marinated beef,mix well & cook on medium flame for 8-10 minutes,cover & cook on low flame until meat is tender (30-35 minutes). -Add fresh coriander,mix well & cook on high flame until it dries up.-Give coal smoke for 2 minutes. -Take a dough ball (100g),sprinkle dry flour & roll out with the help of rolling pin. -Add & spread desi ghee,cut the dough from the center and roll out one edge,twist and again make a dough.-Press gently,sprinkle dry flour & roll out with the help of rolling pin. -On heated griddle,place paratha & fry from both sides with desi ghee.

-Pound beef with the help of meat mallet.
