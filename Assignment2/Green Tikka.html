Green Tikka

Ingredients:

  1. Chicken Tikka 4 pieces
  2. Hara dhania (Fresh coriander) grinded 1 bunch
  3. Dahi (Yogurt) whisked 4 tbs
  4. -Adrak lehsan paste (Ginger garlic paste) 1 tbs
  5. -Kacha papita (Raw papaya) paste 1 & ½ tsp
  6. -Hari mirch (Green chili) crushed 2 tbs
  7. -Zeera powder (Cumin powder) 1 tsp
  8. -Garam masala powder ½ tsp
  9. -Namak (Salt) 2 tsp or to taste
  10. -Lemon juice 2-3 tbs
  11. -Oil 3 tbs
  12. -Pani (Water) as required
  13. Green Masala Sauce:
  14. -Oil 4 tbs
  15. -Hara dhania (Fresh coriander) grinded 1 bunch
  16. -Hari mirch (Green chilies) crushed 5-6
  17. -Dahi (Yogurt) whisked 5-6 tbs
  18. -Garam masala powder ½ tsp
  19. -Namak (Salt) 1 tsp or to taste
  20. -Zeera powder (Cumin powder) ½ tsp
  21. -Kali mirch powder (Black pepper powder) 1 tsp
  22. -Jaifil powder (Nutmeg powder) ¼ tsp
  23. -Koyla (Charcoal) for smoke

Directions:

-Add marks/cuts on chicken tikka pieces. -In bowl,add coriander,yogurt,ginger garlic paste,raw papaya paste,green chili,cumin powder,garam masala powder,salt,lemon juice and mix well. -Add chicken tikka pieces and coat well. -Cover and let it marinate for 4-6 hours or overnight in refrigerator. Option no.1: Grill -Grease grill pan with oil,place marinated chicken tikka pieces and grill for 4-5 minutes each side. -Sprinkle water,cover and cook on low flame until done. Option no.2: Steam -Put chicken tikka pieces into aluminum foil,basting with marinade and wrap it. -Add water in a pot,place steam rack and bring it to boil. -Place wrap piece in steamer rack over water. -Cover and let it steam cook for 20-25 minutes or until done. Option no.3: Bake -Place chicken tikka piece in greased baking tray and brush chicken tikka piece with oil. -Bake in preheated oven at 180 C for 35-40 minutes. Green Masala Sauce: -In wok,add oil,fresh coriander,green chilies and mix well. -Add yogurt,garam masala powder,salt,cumin powder,black pepper powder,nutmeg powder and mix well. -Cook for 2 minutes or until oil separates and mix well continuously. -Add all cooked chicken tikka pieces,mix & coat well with green masala sauce. -Give it a charcoal smoke for 2-3 minutes and serve.
