Mayo Garlic Roll

Ingredients for Chicken Mayo Garlic Roll

  • 2 Cups Wheat Flour Dough
  • Boneless Chicken 400 gms
  • Ginger Garlic Paste 1 tsp
  • Lemon Juice 1 tbsp
  • Salt 1 tsp
  • Red Chilli Powder 1 tsp
  • Garam Masala Powder 1/2 tsp
  • Cumin Powder 1 tsp
  • Yogurt 1/4 Cup
  • Oil 2 tbsp

Ingredients for Mayo Garlic Sauce:

  • Mayonnaise 1/4 Cup
    • Fresh Cream 2 tbsp
  • Garlic Paste 1/2 tsp
  • Salt 1/2 tsp
  • Vinegar 3 tbsp
