Malai Tikka

Ingredients:

  1. Dahi (Yogurt) thick ½ Cup
  2. Cream ½ Cup
  3. -Cheddar cheese grated ½ Cup
  4. -Adrak lehsan paste (Ginger garlic paste) ½ tbs
  5. -Hari mirch (Green chilli) crushed 1 tbs
  6. -Lemon juice 1 tbs
  7. -Garam masala powder ½ tsp
  8. -Namak (Salt) 1 tsp or to taste
  9. -Zeera powder (Cumin powder) 1 tsp
  10. -Safed mirch powder (White pepper powder) ½ tsp
  11. -Kali mirch powder (Black pepper powder) ½ tsp
  12. -Meat tenderizer 1 & ½ tsp
  13. -Kasuri meethi (Dried fenugreek leaves) ½ tbs
  14. -Corn flour 1 tbs
  15. -Chicken 4 pieces (approx. 1 kg)
  16. -Cooking oil 2-3 tbs
  17. -Koyla (Charcoal) for smoke

Directions:

-In bowl,add yogurt,cream,cheddar cheese,ginger garlic paste,green chilli,lemon juice,garam masala powder,salt,cumin powder,white pepper powder,black pepper powder,meat tenderizer,dried fenugreek leaves,corn flour and whisk well. -Add cuts on chicken pieces with the help of the knife. -In marinade,add chicken pieces,rub evenly and marinate for 2-3 hours. Option # 1: Pan fry -In frying pan,add cooking oil and marinated chicken pieces,cover and cook on low flame 20-25 minutes and keep turning sides in between. -Give a charcoal smoke for 2 minutes. Option # 2: Baking -Can be bake in preheated oven at 180 C for 20-25 minutes
